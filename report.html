Introduction

The aim of this study is to provide an insight on the most important features that customer take into consideration when they choose a new smartphone, in order to provide market segments and analyze customers’ tastes. The study has been conducted through the use of a choice-based conjoint analysis on data collected through an online survey thanks to the statistical tool of Multinomial Logistic Regression and Bootstrap prediction.
